How it Works

Technology & Domain topics

Content Curation feature to provide you with a refined list of content based on categories like Digital, Fintech , Cloud , AI and so on.

Explore/Discover content based on key word search , view the content in terms of Relevancy , Popularity & Trending recency.

Social sharing feature to share the content to your social accounts (Linkedin, Twitter & WhatsApp). User can post their up or down votes about content for further content refinement.
